About Me

2 min · Unique Karki

Hey there! 👋 I’m just a guy navigating life’s twists and turns, one day at a time. On the tech front, I’m deeply intrigued by the realms of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, NLP, and Computer Vision. But my interests don’t stop there. I’m equally passionate about strumming away on my guitar, belting out tunes, and delving into existential literature that leaves me pondering, courtesy of Dostoevsky.

When I’m not lost in code or philosophical musings, you’ll find me conquering trail races and pounding the pavement to cover the entirety of the Kathmandu valley rim. I’ve already tackled six trail races and completed several half marathons, with only the last 30 kilometers of the Kathmandu valley rim left to conquer – a challenge I plan on overcoming soon. Beyond running, I thrive on activities like swimming, tennis, and basketball, always pushing myself to new limits.

This website is my canvas, where the worlds of Dostoevsky and ML algorithms collide, giving voice to my scattered thoughts and eclectic interests. Join me on this journey where philosophy meets technology, and let’s explore the intersections of these seemingly disparate worlds together. 🚀
